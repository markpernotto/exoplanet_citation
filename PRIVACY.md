# Privacy

This project ingests and republishes public scientific data about exoplanets
and the publications that announced them. It does not collect, process, or
store any personal information about visitors.

## What we publish

- Confirmed exoplanet records from the NASA Exoplanet Archive (no personal data)
- Bibliographic metadata for scientific publications (titles, authors as listed
  on the publications themselves, DOIs, journal names) — this is public
  scholarly metadata sourced from Crossref, arXiv, and NASA ADS

## What we collect from visitors

Nothing. The public site has no analytics, no cookies, and no user accounts.

## Server logs

The hosting providers (Vercel, Neon, Cloudflare R2) maintain their own
operational logs per their respective privacy policies. We do not aggregate
or analyze visitor logs.
